MY JOURNEY

My interest in web development started during my teenage years, when I built websites using HTML / CSS / Wordpress and Shopify. I started affiliate websites or even my own e-commerce stores, selling fashion items, which I bought at wholesale prices in France, Germany and the UK. But after my websites were built and up & running, I felt the need to keep building a new website .. over and over again.

Through word of mouth, it became known in my circle that I made websites and people started coming to me to build their blogs or online stores using Wordpress and Shopify. At the time, I never pursued development as a career because I didn't fully understand what it meant, as I didn't know anyone who worked as a software developer.

Fast-forward to today! Being fluent in multiple languages, I have been freelancing as a technical translator for the past few years, meaning I translate very difficult language for Fintech or Software companies looking to expand their businesses into new countries.

This is when it all started clicking for me again, I was hired to translate text in between HTML & Javascript codes, since I could clearly understand what was code and what was not. While working on these projects, in the back of my mind I was like hmm interesting, wouldn’t it be cool to start building things again.

This time around the universe told me loud and clear that now was the time to become a developer. Every day I saw advertising campaigns of Tech bootcamps, courses and universities that I could sign up for. So that's exactly what I did, I enrolled in the SheCodes course and I can truly say that I have never enjoyed anything so much in my life.

Today I can finally say that I have found my purpose, this is what I was always meant to do, I just hadn't figured it out yet. Sometimes I wish someone had told me that years ago ... like hey, you should become a Software Developer. But everyone has their own journey and this was my path to walk … the important thing is that im here now.
